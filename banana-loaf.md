# Banana Loaf

## Ingredients

* 8 oz of self-raising flour
* 2 eggs
* a pinch of salt
* 2 tsp of baking powder
* 2 oz of margarine
* 5 oz of carter sugar
* 3 bananas

## Equipment

* bowl
* wooden spoon
* oven
* loaf tin

## Method

1. Get a bowl and beat the sugar and margarine together
2. Mash the bananas into the mix
3. Add the remaining ingredients and mix
4. pour the mix into a loaf tin and cook at 150Â°C for 20 to 30 minutes or until knife comes out cleanly
